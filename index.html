Who We Are

Your Smile is Our Priority

Welcome to Bright Dental! Our office was founded in 2001 and is located in downtown Chicago. From the beginning, our mission has been to provide high-quality dental care to our patients while ensuring they feel comfortable and confident in their smiles.

Over the years, we have grown and expanded our services to meet the changing needs of our patients. Today, our team of skilled dental professionals uses state-of-the-art technology and techniques to offer a wide range of dental services, including preventive care, restorative treatments, and cosmetic procedures.

We take pride in our commitment to patient education and making sure that each of our patients fully understands their treatment options. Our goal is to provide our patients with the tools and knowledge they need to achieve and maintain optimal oral health.

We look forward to serving you and your family!

Albert J. Levy D.D.S.
Bright Dental Founder & Director

See Our Facility

Bright Dental is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and modern facilities to ensure our patients receive the best possible dental care.

Ensuring Your Comfort

Upon entering our office, you will be greeted by our friendly and professional staff, who are always ready to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have. Our reception area is designed to make you feel at ease with comfortable seating and a calm ambiance.

Our treatment rooms are equipped with the latest dental technology, including digital x-ray machines, intraoral cameras, and computer-aided design and manufacturing (CAD/CAM) technology. We also use laser dentistry and air abrasion to provide our patients with the most advanced and minimally invasive treatments available.

Our sterilization and infection control procedures are rigorous and exceed all standards set by the American Dental Association (ADA) and the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA). We use the latest sterilization equipment and techniques to ensure the safety and well-being of our patients.

Minimally Invasive Imaging

Digital X-ray equipment has revolutionized the field of dentistry, allowing for faster and more accurate diagnoses of dental conditions.

With the use of advanced digital imaging technology, Bright Dental is able to obtain high-quality images of the teeth, jawbone, and other oral structures with minimal exposure to radiation. This has significantly improved the safety and comfort of patients, as well as the speed and accuracy of diagnosis and treatment planning.

Bright Dental firmly believes that digital X-ray equipment is an essential tool for modern dentistry, enabling our dentists to provide you with the highest quality care possible.

Say Goodbye to Drills

Laser dentistry is a cutting-edge technology that has transformed the way dental procedures are performed. By using focused beams of light, dentists can now perform a wide range of procedures with minimal pain, bleeding, and discomfort for the patient.

From treating gum disease and cavities to cosmetic procedures like teeth whitening, laser dentistry is a versatile and effective tool that offers numerous benefits over traditional methods. At Bright Dental, we use laser technology to achieve more precise results, reduce the risk of infection, and speed up the healing process.

Laser dentistry is a safe and efficient way to perform many dental procedures, making it an ideal option for patients of all ages. By incorporating laser dentistry into our practice, Bright Dental can provide you with the latest advancements in dental care and improve your overall oral health and well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here's a list of some common questions that our clients ask us. We hope that you find the information helpful and informative. If you have any additional questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to schedule an appointment with us. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always here to assist you.

We recommend that you visit the dentist for a checkup and cleaning at least twice a year, or every six months. However, some patients with certain conditions may need to visit more frequently.

During a dental cleaning, the hygienist will use special tools to remove tartar and plaque from your teeth and gums. They will also polish your teeth to remove surface stains. The dentist may also examine your teeth and gums for any signs of decay or other problems.

A dental crown is a tooth-shaped cap that is placed over a damaged or decayed tooth to restore its shape, size, and strength. A dental crown may be needed to protect a weak or cracked tooth, restore a broken or severely worn tooth, cover a dental implant, or improve the appearance of a misshapen or discolored tooth.

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots that are surgically placed in the jawbone to support a replacement tooth or bridge. They are a popular and effective option for replacing missing teeth.

If you have a dental emergency, such as a toothache or a broken tooth, please call our office immediately. We will do our best to see you as soon as possible and provide the necessary treatment.
